Cradle 2 the Grave is part of the original soundtrack of the 2003 action film Cradle 2 de the Grave, The X Gon Give It To Ya sound is part of the album's flagship tracks.

The production of the album was handled by several record producers including Shatek King, DJ Envy, Dame Grease, Elite, Eminem and many more.

The soundtrack was very successful, reaching No.6 on the Billboard 200, No.3 on Best R & B / Hip-Hop Albums, and No.1 on Best Soundtracks.

Rage Against the Machine is an American rap metal band from Los Angeles, California. Their musical style is mainly a fusion of metal and rap, with funk and punk influences. Composed of Zack from the Rocha, Tom Morello, Tim Commerford and Brad Wilk, the group will mark the years 1990 until its dissolution in 2000. It reformed in January 2007 for the festival of Coachella is the group is back this year on the front of the stage

Rage Against the Machine is characterized mainly by the rhythm of the signed lyrics of La Rocha, the guitar effects of Morello and the political stances in and outside the musical sphere (against the racism, capitalism and globalization). The band is ranked 48th on VH110's "100 Best Artists of All Time" list and 34th on Spin11 Magazine's "50 Best Bands of All Time" list.
